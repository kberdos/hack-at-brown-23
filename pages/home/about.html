Instructions:
- You must have a profile and be logged in to start or join a campfire. To create a profile or sign in, click on the profile button above. Then, click on the home button to see active campfires and join.
- Click on the spark button to start a campfire. You will specify the location, genre and any additional notes about the campfire. Other Brown students will be able to see your jam session, and if interested, join you!

Spark was created at Hack@Brown 2023 by four first-time hackers! We wanted to focus on community and music around the campfire. Pictured left to right: Taj, Kazuya, Rachel, Megan.
